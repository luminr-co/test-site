Broker & CEO

Introducing Behnood (Ben) Dalaei, the visionary Broker at 4ever Lending USA & known as the Mortgage Rescuer. With over a decade of finance industry expertise, Ben's passion lies in delivering unparalleled value to our clients.  His philosophy centers around being the go-to broker for mortgage rescues.  A true master of negotiation, Ben sees numbers as his playground, crafting innovative financing solutions to make homeownership dreams a reality.
At 4ever Lending, Ben's values—speed, accuracy, and
communication—are the pillars of our company ethos. Founded in 2020, we're not your average mortgage brokerage; specializing in rescue loans, we've helped thousands achieve their homeownership goals.  Our track
record speaks volumes: over half a Billion dollars in closed loans, backed by rave reviews and a network of 75 lenders.
But Ben's vision doesn't stop there; he's revolutionizing the industry with the first cloud-based mortgage company, offering unprecedented compensation for our loan officers. Committed to their success, Ben fosters a 'your success is my success' mentality, making himself readily available to the team. Together, we excel in turning challenging situations into successes.

Introducing Nez, our stellar Operations Manager and your Mortgage Bestie! Her commitment to finding creative financing solutions for borrowers sets her apart, making her an invaluable asset for our borrowers & clients.
Nez embodies the ethos of building meaningful connections with a firm belief in the power of genuine relationships. She goes above and beyond to foster trust and understanding with every client. For Nez, it's not just about closing deals; it's about creating lasting partnerships built on integrity.
